To the end

One of the best entertainers out there.

Technoblade (Real name Alexander) was known for his Minecraft videos: Hypixel ones and letsplays, as well as for his awesome taste of humor.

Technoblade died at the age of 23. The reason was cancer.

He have been a big part of Minrcaft community and will be remembered as a bright figure in the history of the game.

You can find out more about this person on his Wikipedea page

His most popular video (exept "So long nerds") is the "Proof I don't autoclock".
